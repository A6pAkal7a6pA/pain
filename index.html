Game Features

Crusade

The rift is open and the underworld army prepares to invade. Lead the campaign, defend our world! Descend to hell, defeat the army of darkness, and rise!

Heroes

Go to hell with a group of crusaders find out what potential is hidden in each of them. Each hero has a unique path of evolving.

Tokens
Memorial
MEMORIAL token - is a DAO token. Designed primarily for investors and holders, but deeply integrated into key game mechanics. We understand that investors are ultimately interested in cash-out. Due to the game mechanics associated with the DAO token, the demand for the MEMORIAL token will grow among the players, and its limited nature and difficulty of mining increases its value.
Pain
PAIN token - game utility token. It serves as the main currency for paying for actions and in-game services, buying and exchanging game goods on the marketplace. PAIN token is of interest primarily to players, without this token it is not possible to fully play the game.
