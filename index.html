Tokenomics
Services
PAIN token is the main game currency. Inside the game, the token is used for all exchange operations and payments for game services. Without a token, a player practically cannot play.
Trading
Buy and sell in-game items on the in-game bourse. Be an active participant in the market, shape the in-game economy. React to changes in the situations or speculate.
Outposts
As the attack on hell progresses, heroes will need safe places to catch their breath before returning to the front lines. Help in the construction of Camps and Outposts, get a share of the property (owner token), and receive daily income from all actions that will take place at this outpost.
Own Bosses
On rare occasions, slew Heroes can embrace dark powers and transform into demonic bosses. Such bosses walk through the dungeons and kill other heroes, taking their souls and items. Thus, players continue to earn in-game assets even after the death of their heroes.
